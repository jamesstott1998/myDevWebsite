Unknown Entities

Mysterious objects are objects that are exhibit unusual, unexpected or malevolent behaviour. A great many objects have been recorded over the centuries. While the government has not formally released a list of recorded objects to the public, a list does exist.

This is that list.

This is a collection of some of the most mysterious objects that have ever been seen on Earth. Objects that have led onto death and destruction wherever they go. Yet are celebrated by the few who seek to know more, to see deeper meaning within the objects.
